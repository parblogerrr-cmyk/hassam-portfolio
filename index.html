01 — About

The Intersection of
Language & Design

I am a 6th-semester English Linguistics and Literature student at Air University, Islamabad. My academic journey is defined by a fascination with how language functions both as a tool for social power and a medium for personal expression.

Currently, my work focuses on the intersection of Discourse Studies and Creative Non-Fiction. Through Discourse Studies, I explore the underlying ideologies and structures within texts; through Creative Non-Fiction, I apply those insights to craft compelling, true-to-life narratives. This portfolio is a curated collection of my journey—blending critical linguistic analysis with the art of storytelling to better understand the world we inhabit.
